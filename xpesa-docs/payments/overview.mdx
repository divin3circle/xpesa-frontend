---
title: "Payment Overview"
description: "How money moves from fan to creator."
icon: "arrows-left-right"
---

xpesa lets fans pay creators in dollars using USDC. After payment, the creator receives the payment in their wallet, and the fan gets access to the content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fan opens checkout">
    The fan opens a creator's xpesa link and sees the price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fan pays in USDC">
    The fan approves the payment with a wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="xpesa verifies the payment">
    xpesa checks that the payment completed successfully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Content unlocks">
    The fan receives access according to the creator's settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Creator withdraws">
    The creator can withdraw available earnings to M-Pesa where supported.
  </Step>
</Steps>
