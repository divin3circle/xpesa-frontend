Your handle is the short public name people use to find your creator page.

For example, the handle `amina` gives you:

```txt
{exampleCreatorPageUrl}
```

## Handle rules

- Use a short name that matches your creator identity.
- Use letters, numbers, and simple separators where supported.
- Avoid names that look like another creator, brand, or organization.
- Keep it easy to say out loud and easy to type.

Your creator page follows this pattern: `{creatorPageUrlPattern}`.

<Note>
  You can change your handle later, but changing it may break links you already shared.
</Note>
