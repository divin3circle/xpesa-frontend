## Privacy Policy 

Last updated: May 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how XPesa Creators ("XPesa", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information about you when you use our platform at XPesacreators.xyz (the "Platform"). By using XPesa, you agree to the practices described in this policy. 

## 1. Who We Are 

XPesa Creators is an early-stage creator monetization platform operated by an individual founder based in Kenya. We enable creators to monetize digital content and links, and facilitate payments in USDC on the Avalanche blockchain with M-Pesa withdrawal support. We are not currently a registered legal entity; formal registration is in progress. Our contact email is info@XPesacreators.xyz. 

## 2. Information We Collect 

## 2.1 Information You Provide Directly 

- Full name and display name 

- Email address 

- Profile photo / avatar 

- Bio and handle (your public username) 

- M-Pesa phone number (for withdrawal purposes only) 

- Wallet address (your Avalanche-compatible wallet) 

- Content you upload (documents, images, files) and links you create

## 5. How We Share Your Information 

## 5.1 With Other Users 

Your public creator profile (display name, handle, bio, avatar, and active links) is visible to anyone who visits your creator page. Your wallet address is shared with fans in the context of payment verification but is not displayed publicly on your profile. 

## 5.2 With Service Providers 

We share data with the following third-party service providers who process data on our behalf: 

- Supabase (Ireland/US): database, authentication, and file storage 

- ThirdWeb (US): wallet connection and smart account infrastructure 

- Kotani Pay (Kenya): M-Pesa offramp and currency conversion 

- Upstash (US): Redis caching for session and access token management 

- Avalanche Network: public blockchain for payment settlement 

- Anthropic (US): AI-powered link description generation (Claude API) 

Each service provider is bound by data processing agreements and may only use your data for the specific purposes we have engaged them for. 

## 5.3 Legal Requirements 

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of XPesa, our users, or the public. 

## 5.4 Business Transfers 

If XPesa is acquired, merged, or undergoes a change in ownership, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy. 

## 6. Data Retention 

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically: 

- Account data: retained until account deletion, then 30 days for backup purposes 

- Transaction records: retained for 7 years for financial record-keeping purposes 

- Access tokens: deleted automatically when they expire (per the expiry you set) 

- Uploaded content: deleted within 30 days of link deletion 

- IP address hashes: retained for 90 days for fraud detection 

- Blockchain data: permanent — cannot be deleted from the public blockchain

## 7. Data Security 

We implement the following security measures to protect your data: 

- Gate link destination URLs are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM 

- Uploaded documents and file packs are stored in a private storage bucket with no public access 

- Content is served via short-lived signed URLs (30-second expiry) — never via permanent public links 

- Access tokens are wallet-bound — only the wallet that paid can access the content 

- All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 

- Mobile numbers are stored encrypted and only used for withdrawal processing 

Despite these measures, no internet transmission or storage system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

- Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@XPesacreators.xyz. We will respond within 30 days. Note that some data (blockchain transactions) cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of public blockchains.
