---
title: "How to Pay for Content"
description: "Buy and unlock xpesa content without a full account."
icon: "credit-card"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the creator's link">
    Visit the xpesa link the creator shared.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect or create a wallet">
    Use an existing wallet, or create an email-based embedded wallet if you are new.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the payment">
    Review the price and approve the USDC payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the content">
    After the payment confirms, xpesa unlocks the link, document, file pack, or tip confirmation page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You do not need a creator account to buy content.
</Note>
