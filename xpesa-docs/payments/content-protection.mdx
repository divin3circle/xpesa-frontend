---
title: "Content Protection"
description: "How xpesa protects documents, packs, and paid links."
icon: "shield"
---

xpesa protects content by combining payment verification with access rules set by the creator.

## Documents

Documents can be viewed through xpesa instead of exposing a raw file URL. Creators can add max views, wallet binding, and wallet watermarks.

## File packs

Packs are only available after a successful payment. Access can be tied to the paying wallet and limited by expiry.

## Gate links

Gate links reveal the destination only after payment. They work best when paired with expiry, wallet binding, or one-time access for sensitive content.

## Signed URLs

For protected files, xpesa can use temporary secure links to serve content without exposing permanent storage URLs.

<Note>
  Content protection reduces casual sharing, but no online protection method can guarantee that a determined buyer will never copy what they can view.
</Note>
