Your creator page is the public home for your paid links. Fans can see your profile, bio, and available offers from one URL.

## What fans see

- Your display name and handle
- Your avatar and short bio
- Active links, documents, packs, and tips
- Prices and payment buttons

## What you can customize

You can update your display name, bio, avatar, and active links from your dashboard.

## Sharing your page

Your public page uses the format `{creatorPageUrlPattern}`. Add it to your social bio, email footer, community profile, or post captions.
