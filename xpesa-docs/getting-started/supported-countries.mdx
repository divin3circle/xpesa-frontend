## Fan payments

Fans can pay from anywhere if they have a compatible crypto wallet with USDC on a supported network.

## What this means

Creators should confirm that their M-Pesa number is active before requesting a withdrawal. Fans outside Kenya can still buy content as long as they can complete the USDC payment.
