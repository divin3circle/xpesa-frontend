When a creator withdraws, USDC is converted into local currency and sent to the creator's M-Pesa number through an offramp partner.

## Kotani Pay

Kotani Pay helps handle the conversion from USDC to mobile money. You may see their name in the withdrawal flow.

## Rate and timing

The KES amount depends on the rate shown when you request the withdrawal. The current minimum withdrawal is {minimumWithdrawal}. Withdrawals are {withdrawalTimeline}.

<Warning>
  Always confirm the M-Pesa number before submitting a withdrawal.
