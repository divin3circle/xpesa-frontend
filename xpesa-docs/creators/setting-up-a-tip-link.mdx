A tip link lets fans support you without buying a fixed product.

## Amount options

Use a suggested amount when you want to guide fans toward a fair contribution. Use pay-what-you-want when the tip is open-ended.

## Thank-you message

Add a short thank-you message that appears after payment. Keep it personal and clear.

## Where to share it

- Social bio
- Newsletter footer
- End of posts
- Video descriptions
- Community announcements

<Tip>
  Tip links work well when fans already know your work and want a simple way to support it.
</Tip>
