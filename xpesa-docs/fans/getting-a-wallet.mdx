A wallet is what you use to pay and prove that you bought the content.

## If you are new

Use the embedded wallet option. You sign in with email, and xpesa creates the wallet experience for you.

## If you already use crypto

Connect your existing wallet during checkout. Make sure it has enough USDC on the supported network.

<Tip>
  If you are unsure which option to pick, choose the email wallet.
</Tip>
