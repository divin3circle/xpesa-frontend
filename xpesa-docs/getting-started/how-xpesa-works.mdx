---
title: "How xpesa Works"
description: "The full xpesa flow in plain language."
icon: "route"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Creator makes a link">
    The creator creates a gate link, uploads a document, creates a file pack, or sets up a tip link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Creator shares it">
    They share the link on a public creator page, social bio, DM, email, community, or post.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fan pays">
    The fan opens the link, connects or creates a wallet, and pays the displayed price in USDC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Content unlocks">
    xpesa confirms the payment and gives the fan access to the link, document, file pack, or thank-you page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Creator withdraws to M-Pesa">
    The creator withdraws their available USDC balance to an M-Pesa number in supported markets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Fans do not need a full xpesa account to buy content.
</Note>
