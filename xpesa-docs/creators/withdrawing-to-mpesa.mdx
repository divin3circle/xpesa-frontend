Creators can withdraw available USDC earnings to an M-Pesa number in supported markets.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open withdrawals">
    Go to your dashboard wallet area and choose withdraw.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount">
    Enter the USDC amount you want to withdraw. The current minimum is {minimumWithdrawal}.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the M-Pesa number">
    Enter the M-Pesa number that should receive the payout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the conversion">
    Check the KES estimate and payout details before confirming.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm withdrawal">
    Submit the withdrawal and wait for the M-Pesa payout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What is Kotani Pay?

Kotani Pay is the offramp partner that helps convert USDC into local mobile money payouts. You may see their name during the withdrawal flow or in payout references.
