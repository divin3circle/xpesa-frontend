## How xpesa protects your content

xpesa stores uploaded files privately and only unlocks them after successful payment. Documents can use viewer-only access, wallet binding, max views, and watermarking.

## How xpesa handles your money

xpesa is non-custodial. Creator payments go to the creator's wallet, and withdrawals move available USDC through the supported offramp path.

## Privacy

xpesa stores the data needed to operate accounts, links, payments, access controls, and withdrawals. Fan wallet addresses may be used for payment verification, access control, and watermarking.

## Platform fees and transparency

xpesa charges a {platformFee} platform fee on completed payments. This supports platform operations, storage, development, and integrations.

<Note>
  xpesa should only request the information needed to deliver paid content and process withdrawals.
</Note>
