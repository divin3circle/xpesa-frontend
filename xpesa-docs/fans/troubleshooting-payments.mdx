---
title: "Troubleshooting Payments"
description: "Common payment issues and simple fixes."
icon: "triangle-alert"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wrong network selected" icon="plug">
    Switch your wallet to the network shown in checkout, then try the payment again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Insufficient USDC balance" icon="circle-dollar-sign">
    Add enough USDC to cover the content price, then refresh checkout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transaction failed" icon="x-circle">
    Try again after checking your wallet balance and network. If the payment still fails, contact support with the link and wallet address.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transaction pending" icon="loader">
    Wait for the network to confirm the payment. Pending transactions can take longer during network congestion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Content not loading after payment" icon="file-question">
    Refresh the page, reconnect the same wallet, and check that your access has not expired.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
