A wallet is like a digital account you control. It receives your creator payments and signs actions that prove you are the owner.

## Option 1: Create a wallet with email

If you are new to crypto, use the embedded wallet option. You sign in with email, and xpesa creates a wallet experience in the background.

## Option 2: Connect an existing wallet

If you already use a wallet, connect it during onboarding. Make sure it supports the network and token xpesa uses for payments.

<Note>
  xpesa is non-custodial. Creator payments go to your wallet; xpesa does not hold your funds.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Keep access to your wallet and email secure. If you lose access to both, recovering your creator wallet may be difficult.
</Warning>
