Document mode lets you upload a file and sell viewer-based access instead of revealing a raw URL.

## Supported files

- PDF
- DOCX

## Why use document mode

Document mode is better than a gate link when you do not want the final file URL exposed. Fans view the content through xpesa, and you can add controls like wallet binding, max views, and watermarking.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose document upload">
    Open your dashboard and choose document mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the file">
    Select a supported PDF or DOCX file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set price and access">
    Add the price, description, expiry, view limits, and optional watermarking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Share the generated payment link with your audience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Use PDF for finished resources and DOCX when your audience expects an editable template.
</Note>
