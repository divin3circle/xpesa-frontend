title: "Creating a Gate Link"
description: "Sell access to a private URL."

---

<img src="/images/links.avif" alt="Paid link illustration" />

A gate link lets you sell access to a private destination URL. After a fan pays, xpesa reveals or redirects them to the content.

Good destinations include:

- Google Drive folders
- Notion pages
- Zoom or Calendly links
- Telegram or WhatsApp community links
- Private landing pages

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open link creation">
    From your dashboard, choose the option to create a new link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the destination URL">
    Paste the private URL fans should receive after payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the price">
    Price in dollars. For early offers, start with a simple amount fans can understand quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose access settings">
    Pick expiry, one-time access, wallet binding, or IP binding if the content needs stronger protection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and share">
    Copy the xpesa link and share it wherever your audience already finds you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Gate links work best for content already hosted somewhere else.
</Tip>
