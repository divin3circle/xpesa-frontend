USDC is a dollar-equivalent stablecoin. One USDC is designed to be worth one US dollar.

## Where to get USDC

You can buy USDC through exchanges or wallet providers such as Coinbase, Binance, or supported on-ramp services.

## What to check

- You have enough USDC for the content price.
- Your USDC is on the network xpesa supports for checkout.
- You have enough balance before opening the payment page.

<Note>
  If you are using an email-based embedded wallet, the checkout flow may guide you through the easiest available funding option.
</Note>
