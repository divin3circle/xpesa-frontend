xpesa charges a {platformFee} platform fee on completed payments.

<Card title="Example" icon="calculator">
  If a fan pays $10, the creator receives $9.50.
</Card>

The fee is deducted when the payment completes. There are no hidden xpesa fees added later.

## What the fee supports

- Platform operations
- Storage and delivery infrastructure
- Product development
- Payment and withdrawal integrations
