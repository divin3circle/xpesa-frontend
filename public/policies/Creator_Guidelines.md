## Creator Community Guidelines 

These guidelines set the standard for what creators can publish on XPesa Creators. They exist to protect creators, buyers, and the platform. Violations may result in content removal or account suspension and/or legal action. 

## 1. Content Standards 

## 1.1 Be Honest About What You Are Selling 

Your link title and description must accurately represent what the buyer will receive. Do not use misleading thumbnails, exaggerated claims, or fake social proof. If your content is a preview, a template, or requires additional purchases to be useful, say so clearly. 

## 1.2 Deliver What You Promise 

Buyers pay based on what you describe. If a destination URL stops working, a Google Drive folder becomes inaccessible, or content is removed, you should replace it promptly or communicate with affected buyers. 

## 1.3 Price Fairly 

There is no required pricing on XPesa. However, we encourage fair and transparent pricing. Charging for free public content (e.g. gating a Wikipedia link or a publicly available PDF) is a violation of these guidelines. 

## 2. Prohibited Categories 

The following content categories are prohibited on XPesa regardless of legality in any jurisdiction: 

- Adult content, pornography, or sexually explicit material of any kind 

- Content featuring minors in any inappropriate, suggestive, or exploitative context 

- Malware, phishing links, or any content designed to harm buyers' devices or data 

- Pirated content — books, films, software, music you do not own rights to 

- Personal data of individuals sold without their consent 

- Fake certificates, degrees, or official documents 

- Content that enables or promotes financial fraud or scams 

- Hate speech targeting race, religion, gender, ethnicity, disability, or sexual orientation 

- Content that glorifies violence or terrorism

## 3. Intellectual Property 

Only sell content you created or have explicit permission to sell. This includes: 

- Do not resell other creators' XPesa content without their written permission 

- • Do not gate access to content owned by third parties without their authorization 

- Respect copyright in all uploaded documents, images, and linked resources 

## 4. Community Standards 

## 4.1 Authenticity 

Be genuine. Do not create fake buyer accounts to inflate your payment count, purchase your own content, or manufacture social proof. 

## 4.2 Respect for Buyers 

Treat your buyers with respect. Do not use buyer contact information (if obtained through XPesa interactions) to spam, harass, or market to them without their consent. 

## 4.3 Transparency About Crypto 

If your audience is new to cryptocurrency, help them understand the payment process rather than rushing them through it. Buyers should feel confident, not confused, when paying on XPesa. 

## 5. Reporting Violations 

If you encounter content on XPesa that you believe violates these guidelines, please report it to info@XPesacreators.com with the link URL and a description of the concern. We investigate all reports. 

## 6. Enforcement 

When we identify a violation, we may take the following actions depending on severity: 

- Warning: for first-time minor violations 

- Content removal: the specific link is deactivated 

- Account suspension: temporary restriction of your account 

- Permanent ban: for severe or repeated violations 

- Legal actions 

We will endeavour to notify you of enforcement actions and give you an opportunity to appeal where appropriate. Decisions on account termination are final in cases involving illegal content.
