USDC is a stablecoin. That means it is designed to keep a stable value instead of moving up and down like many crypto assets.

xpesa uses USDC because creators can price content in dollars, fans can understand the amount clearly, and earnings can later be converted to local currency during withdrawal.

## How it relates to KES

Creators earn in USDC, then withdraw through the M-Pesa offramp. The KES amount depends on the conversion rate shown during withdrawal.

<Note>
  Always review the displayed conversion before confirming a withdrawal.
</Note>
