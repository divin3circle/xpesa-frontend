## Cookie Policy 

Last updated: May 2026. This Cookie Policy explains how XPesa Creators use cookies and similar technologies on xpesacreators.xyz 

## 1. What Are Cookies 

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners. 

## 2. Cookies We Use 

## 2.1 Essential Cookies 

These cookies are necessary for XPesa to function and cannot be disabled. 

- Authentication session cookie: keeps you logged in as a creator 

- CSRF protection token: prevents cross-site request forgery attacks 

- Preview session cookie (HTTP-only): enables secure content access after payment 

## 2.2 Functional Cookies 

These cookies remember your preferences and improve your experience. 

- Wallet connection state: remembers your connected wallet for the session 

- Theme preference: remembers your display preferences 

## 2.3 Analytics 

We use privacy-preserving, aggregated analytics to understand how creators use the Platform. We do not use Google Analytics or any advertising analytics. No individual user data is shared with analytics providers. 

## 3. What We Do NOT Use 

- Advertising cookies or tracking pixels 

- Third-party remarketing or retargeting cookies 

- Social media tracking cookies 

- Fingerprinting or persistent cross-site tracking

## 4. Session Storage 

XPesa uses browser sessionStorage (not cookies) to temporarily store unlocked content state during your session. This data is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab and is never transmitted to our servers. 

## 5. Managing Cookies 

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging into XPesa as a creator. Fans who pay without an account are not affected by cookie settings beyond their session authentication.
