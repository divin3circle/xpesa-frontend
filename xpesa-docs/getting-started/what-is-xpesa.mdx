{productName} is a monetization platform for African creators selling digital content, paid sessions, private links, documents, packs, and tips. Creators can price content in dollars, receive USDC, and withdraw earnings to M-Pesa.

It exists for creators who already sell through DMs, links, forms, and manual payment screenshots, but want a cleaner flow: one checkout, automatic access, and a payout path that makes sense locally.
