Your dashboard shows how your paid content is performing.

## Main stats

- **Total earned**: lifetime USDC received from completed payments.
- **This month**: earnings in the current calendar month.
- **Transactions**: total completed payments.
- **Active links**: live published offers fans can still access.

## Transaction history

Transaction history shows each completed payment, the paid offer, amount, date, and buyer wallet details where available.

## Link stats

- **Views** show how many people opened the payment page.
- **Payments** show how many completed checkout.
- **Conversion** shows the percentage of viewers who paid.

<Tip>
  If views are high but payments are low, test clearer titles, simpler pricing, or a stronger preview of what fans get.
</Tip>
