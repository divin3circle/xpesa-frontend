---
title: "Accessing Content After Payment"
description: "Return to content while your access is still valid."
icon: "unlock"
---

After payment, xpesa unlocks the purchased content for the wallet that paid.

## Returning later

Open the original xpesa link again and connect the same wallet you used to pay. If your access window is still active, the content will open.

## Expired access

If the creator set an expiry and your access window has ended, you may need to pay again or contact the creator.

## Document watermarking

Some documents show your wallet address as a watermark. This helps creators discourage unauthorized sharing.

<Warning>
  If wallet binding is enabled, a different wallet will not be able to open the same purchase.
</Warning>
